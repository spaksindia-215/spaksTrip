Important Implementations

We have added below 2 nodes:

  • BeneficiaryTitle

    • To ensure the integrity and accuracy of passenger information for insurance bookings, the following validation rules must be applied:

    1.Beneficiary Name Format:

    The BeneficiaryName must be in the proper format, including:

  • Title: e.g., Mr., Mrs., Ms.
  • FirstName: e.g., John
  • LastName: e.g., Doe

    • Example: Mr. John Doe

    2.Beneficiary Name Length:

  • The **FirstName** of the beneficiary must be greater than 2 letters.
  • The **LastName** of the beneficiary must also be greater than 2 letters.

    • 3.Phone Number Format:

  • The **PhoneNumber** for the passenger must consist of exactly 10 digits.
  • Example: 1234567890

    • 4.Pin Code Format:

  • The **PinCode** must be exactly 6 digits.
  • Example: 110001
